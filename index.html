Semey

Semey (also spelled Semei; former Semipalatinsk, renamed in 2007) is a city of Abay region of Kazakhstan standing on Irtysh river banks.


  1. Semey is one the most ancient cities of Kazakhstan. Semey city is an important railway junction connecting Russia with Eastern and Southern Kazakhstan.

  2. Semey city has airport and river port.

  3. Semey population is about 330,000 (2009).
